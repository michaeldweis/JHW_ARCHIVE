Historical Context

Timeline

  • 1964

    John H. Weis is documented in formal electronics training. This period coincides with the transition into solid-state electronics and the launch era of IBM System/360.

  • 1966

    System/360 utility documentation appears in the archive, including DEBE utility material dated February 24, 1966.

  • 1967-1968

    The binder label places the main archive group in this period. The materials include printer-image demonstrations, programming notes, card/tape references, and line-printer output.

  • Late 1960s-1970s

    The archive reflects operational computing culture: card decks, tape-to-printer routines, utility programs, EBCDIC/hex references, and systems-level logic.

  • Preservation period

    The materials survive in family custody as a coherent technical and visual archive connected to John H. Weis.
